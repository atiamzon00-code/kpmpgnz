Mission & Vision

Mission: Matenakan's mission is to create reliable and meaningful solutions that people can trust, rooted in the ingenuity of Kapampangan craftsmanship and innovation. That makes technology and progress accessible and relevant, while supporting local enterprises, empowering communities, and promoting sustainable growth. By combining tradition with innovation, the company strives to uplift lives, preserve cultural identity, and shape a better tomorrow for Pampanga and beyond.

Vision:A leading Kapampangan company that champions innovation while honoring rich cultural heritage, aims to improve everyday living, strengthen local communities, and open opportunities that showcase Pampanga's creativity and resilience.

Brand Story

KPMPNGN is a mobile application that aims to promote Pampanga as a premier cultural and tourism destination in the Philippines. The app provides comprehensive information on historical sites, local cuisines, festivals, accommodations, and hidden travel spots. Equipped with interactive maps, where-to go spots, and user-friendly navigation, KPMPNGN enhances the travel experience for both local and international tourists in Pampanga. Beyond convenience, the application serves as a digital platform to preserve and promote Kapampangan heritage, artistry, and hospitality, supporting sustainable tourism and local economic growth. The Kapampangan App (KPMPNGN) is a comprehensive software application that celebrates the rich culture and heritage of Pampanga, Philippines. The app is designed to cater to the needs of both locals and tourists, providing a wealth of information on the region's location, history & Culture, and attractions
